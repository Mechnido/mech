Paper Submission Guidelines

 
  • The submitted article must include title, author affiliation with email, address, abstract, and keywords.
  • Limit the Abstract within 250 words.
  • Enter Keywords, maximum 6 words.
  • Paper should be submitted as per template, which is available on conference webpage
  • Read the instructions for how to use template
  • Articles should present novel perspectives within the theme of the conference.
  • The paper should not contain page numbers or any special headers or footers.
  • The paper length should not exceed 8 pages .
  • Review papers will be considered if it has been demonstrating sufficient novelty and evidently discussed the benefits in the field. To publish, such papers must go beyond a review of the literature and define the field in a new way or highlights exciting new technologies in the area of research.
  • All submitted papers will be subjected to a similarity test (< 20% similarity.
  • Papers achieving a < 20% similarity score will be examined and those that are deemed unacceptable may be rejected / withdrawn without a formal review.

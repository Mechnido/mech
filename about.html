ABOUT US

ABOUT US

M/S MECHNIDO is an emerging enterprise started in 2018 at Coimbatore, Tamilnadu, primarily to cater the captive needs of local industry.

Early in our inception, we carved a unique business model to success With the state of the art infrastructure, a team of young & energetic ENGINEERS and wide range of products with excellence in quality, working in the field of design AND fabrication of automobile parts.

We are customizing motorsports parts as per the requirements of customers which will match their design, comfort, and priority AS WELL.

MISSION

To offer a sustainable self transportation possible for every citizen by making low cost electric vehicles.

Being environmentally focused company that exceeds Statutory and Customer requirements.

To inculcate knowledge on electric vehicles for every students through workshops.

VISION

MECHNIDO aims to shine as a leading manufacturer of E- mobility and offer creative solutions in the field of automobiles.

World drives through our products

Driving the world by our products.

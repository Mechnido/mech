Elsevier Publishing

Materials Today: Proceedings (Elsevier, Scopus Indexed) Journal is published by Elsevier Ltd. & is available on Science Direct. It provides the materials science community an opportunity to publish research papers in the field of materials science. This Journal is indexed in 3 international databases, so all published articles in this Journal can be read and cited by researchers worldwide. In ICDMME 2021, the Editors are fully responsible for the quality control, the peer review process and the content of papers. All papers must be original, and will be peer-reviewed. The articles submitted to ICDMME 2021 must comply with the same ethical standards as all other academic articles. Permission should be sought to reuse (if any) figures/tables/text from other sources. The article must be prepared as per the manuscript template. Before submission, the authors must ensure that the plagiarism is below 15%.

Call for Papers

 

Call For Papers

 

Authors from India and abroad are invited to submit original unpublished research articles relevant to the theme of the ICDMME 2021 conference. ICDMME 2021 endeavor to publish high quality original research papers, review papers, case studies, and technical notes. All papers in the conference will have to undergo rigorous peer review process.
